Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

Underweight BMI < 18.5

You may need to gain weight. Consult with our healthcare professionals.

Normal Weight BMI 18.5 - 24.9

You're in a healthy weight range. Maintain your current lifestyle.

Overweight BMI 25 - 29.9

Consider lifestyle changes. We can help with weight management plans.
