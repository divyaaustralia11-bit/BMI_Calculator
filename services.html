Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions for your entire family

General Practice

Our experienced general practitioners provide comprehensive medical care for patients of all ages. We offer routine check-ups, diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic conditions, health assessments, and ongoing management of your health needs.

  • General health consultations
  • Chronic disease management
  • Preventive health care
  • Medical certificates
  • Prescription management

Immunizations

Stay protected with our complete immunization services. We provide vaccinations for children, adults, and travelers, following the National Immunisation Program Schedule and WHO recommendations.

  • Childhood vaccinations
  • Adult immunizations
  • Travel vaccines
  • Flu shots (seasonal)
  • COVID-19 vaccinations

Family Health

Comprehensive healthcare services designed for the whole family. From newborns to seniors, we provide personalized care that addresses the unique health needs of each family member.

  • Child health checks
  • Women's health services
  • Men's health programs
  • Elderly care
  • Family planning

Health Screenings

Early detection is key to preventing serious health conditions. We offer a range of health screening services to help identify potential health issues before they become serious.

  • Blood pressure monitoring
  • Diabetes screening
  • Cholesterol tests
  • Cancer screenings
  • Overall health assessments

Minor Procedures

We perform various minor medical procedures in the comfort of our clinic, eliminating the need for hospital visits for routine procedures.

  • Wound care and suturing
  • Skin tag and mole removal
  • Minor surgical procedures
  • Ear syringing
  • ECG and spirometry

Mental Health Support

Mental health is just as important as physical health. We provide supportive mental health services including counseling, mental health treatment plans, and referrals to specialists when needed.

  • Mental health assessments
  • Depression and anxiety management
  • Stress management
  • Mental health care plans
  • Specialist referrals
